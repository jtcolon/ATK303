Let's talk about Informer

How did this song change my life?

"Informer" by Snow was the song that gave me life... literally! I was not even born yet, and suddenly, in March of 1993, this song bypassed "A Whole New World" from Disney's Aladdin and Whitney Houston's "I Will Always Love You" on the Billboard Hot 100. Freaking Whitney Houston and Disney! Is this a coincidence? I think not!

So if Informer rose to the top of the charts only a few short weeks before my birth, this must be a sign, right? There is no way I just happen to be born at the right time. Wow! I never knew how important one song could truly be to me.
